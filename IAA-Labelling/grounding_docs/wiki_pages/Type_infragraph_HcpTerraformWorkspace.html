HCP Terraform vs. Terraform CLI Workspaces

Source: hcp_documentation.md (hcp) Both HCP Terraform and Terraform CLI have features called workspaces, but they function differently. - HCP Terraform workspaces are required. They represent all of the collections of infrastructure in an organization. They are also a major component of role-based access in HCP Terraform. You can grant individual users and user groups permissions for one or more workspaces that dictate whether they can manage variables, perform runs, etc. You cannot manage resources in HCP Terraform without creating at least one workspace.
