Concert risk score components

Source: concert-2.2.x-documentation.md (concert) The Concert risk score is calculated by multiplying three key components: Severity: Measures the technical risk of the CVE, based on the CVSS (Common Vulnerability Scoring System) score. Exploitability: Measures the weaponization potential of the CVE, derived from the EPSS (Exploit Prediction Scoring System) score. Environmental: Measures the risk of the CVE in your specific environment. The Concert risk score is calculated as follows: Concert Risk Score= Severity Score (CVSS) × Exploitability Factor (EPSS) × Environmental Factor The score is normalized to a 10-point scale.
