Managing a compliance catalog

Source: concert-2.2.x-documentation.md (concert) You can upload compliance catalogs to IBM® Concert to streamline the processing and prioritization of compliancerelated results across your applications and environments. Also, you can delete catalogs from Concert when they are no longer needed. Compliance catalogs are collections of security or privacy controls that define specific aspects of an information system. These controls vary depending on the context of the compliance requirements or guidelines. By uploading these catalogs to Concert, you can efficiently manage and prioritize compliance-related results. In the context of compliance scans, low, medium, and high baselines represent the severity levels of compliance violations that are deemed acceptable or tolerable. These baselines help you to set the appropriate level of scrutiny for your organization's compliance scans to make sure that you strike a balance between security and operational efficiency. Compliance catalogs are published by standard or regulatory bodies.
